What we do

Four crafts, one workshop

Every commission begins with a conversation — about dimensions, fabric, comfort and style — and ends with furniture you'll keep for decades.

Re-Upholstery

Full re-upholstery in your choice of fabric or leather — new springs, webbing, foams and finishing, tailored to how you actually sit.

Bespoke Sewing

Cushions, covers, piping and trims sewn in-house — precise, hard-wearing seamwork made to match or transform your furniture.

Fabric Selection

Call into the studio and choose from our fabric library — wools, velvets, linens and hard-wearing weaves from Ireland's and Europe's best mills.

Furniture Repair

Damaged frames, torn seams and pet-loved corners — we mend the damage and apply your chosen fabric so the piece comes back stronger.

The craft

How a piece moves through the studio

  1. 01

    Consultation & assessment

    Bring us the piece, a photo, or just the idea. We assess frame, springs and history, then talk dimensions, fabric, comfort and style — with honest advice on what's worth doing.

  2. 02

    Strip back to the frame

    Old covers, tacks and tired fillings come away by hand. What remains is the bones of the piece — and a clear picture of everything it needs.

  3. 03

    Rebuild & upholster

    Joints are re-glued, springs re-tied, foams and fillings renewed. Then your chosen fabric is cut, matched and hand-finished with proper tension in every panel.

  4. 04

    Finish & deliver

    Polished legs, piping, studs and final details — then your piece comes home, ready for another generation of everyday life.
